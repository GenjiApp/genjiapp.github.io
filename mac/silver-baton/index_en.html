Silver Baton shows a control panel for Music.app whenever you tap a trackpad with multi fingers or click right/middle mouse button. The control panel shows current track info:

  • Track name
  • Album name
  • Artist name
  • Rate
  • Artwork
  • Sound volume (of Music.app or system main volume)
  • Playing position

and controls:

  • Play/pause
  • Move to previous/next track
  • Sound Volume (of Music.app or system main volume)
  • Rating
  • Changing playing position

System Requirements

macOS Catalina 10.15+

Usage

Grant Access

Silver Baton requires to grant access to Input Monitoring and Automation in Security and Pravacy preferences, located in System Preferences.

You must check the checkboxes shown at above preferences.

Show the control panel

To show Silver Baton control panel:

  • Right double click
  • Middle click
  • Middle double click
  • 3-finger tap
  • 3-finger double tap
  • 4-finger tap
  • 4-finger double tap

You can choose one of above methods at Silver Baton's preferences window. Click a bat icon in menu bar. The default method is 4-finger tap.

Show or hide icon

You can choose to show or hide bat icon in menu bar. If the icon is hidden, a gear icon is shown at corner of the control panel. You can open Silver Baton's preferences window by clicking the gear icon.
