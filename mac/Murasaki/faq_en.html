Murasaki FAQ

Cannot open sidebar

If you cannot open sidebar by clicking "Show Sidebar" in "View" on menubar or clicking "Sidebar" button on toolbar, try to remove ~/Library/Preferences/jp.genji.Murasaki.plist and relaunch Murasaki.

  1. Select "Go to Folder..." in "Go" on menubar of Finder
  2. "Go to the folder" dialog appears. Type ~/Library/Preferences in text field and click "Go" button
  3. Remove jp.genji.Murasaki.plist in that folder
  4. Relaunch Murasaki
